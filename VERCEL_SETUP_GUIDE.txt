STEP 4: SET FRONTEND ENVIRONMENT VARIABLES
========================================

1. Go to: https://vercel.com/dashboard
2. Click your frontend project: "tasklist"
3. Click "Settings" tab
4. Click "Environment Variables" on left menu
5. Add this variable:

Name: VITE_API_URL
Value: [PASTE THE BACKEND URL YOU COPIED IN STEP 2]

[Click Save]
